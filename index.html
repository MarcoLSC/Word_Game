How to Play Word Morph

Game Objective

Create the longest possible chain of valid English words by changing one letter at a time or adding/removing letters. Each word must be a real English word.

Basic Rules

  • Click any letter to modify it
  • Use arrow buttons to add letters before or after the selected position
  • Use backspace to remove the selected letter
  • Each word must be a valid English word
  • You can't use the same word twice

Features

  • Click "Chain Length" to view your word history
  • Click "Possible Words" to see hints and statistics
  • Your progress is automatically saved
  • Use the Roll Back feature to return to a previous word

Tips

  • Try adding or removing letters when you're stuck
  • Look for common word patterns and prefixes/suffixes
  • Use the definition hints to verify word meanings
  • Check the possible words count to know if you're on the right track
